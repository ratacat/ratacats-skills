# PM Research Methodologies

A general-purpose research methodology framework for structuring deep research on a subject that has no dedicated methodology skill of its own.

This skill is **manual-only**: it never auto-loads or auto-selects from task context. Load it only when you explicitly name `pm-research-methodologies`, or when you ask for the general research methodology framework to scaffold deep research on a new subject.

Good fits:

- Structuring research on a subject with no existing playbook
- Establishing a repeatable methodology before diving into sources
- Providing a reusable backbone for one-off deep research

If a subject already has a dedicated methodology skill, prefer that skill over this general framework.
